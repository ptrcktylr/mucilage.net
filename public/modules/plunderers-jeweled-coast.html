Plunderers of the Jeweled Coast

A pulpy, swashbuckling mini-adventure in search of an ancient queen's priceless treasure.

Description

Any scoundrel with a blade and a boat is racing toward the Jeweled Coast, seeking their fortune from the ruins of a pre-Elven empire. The most coveted among the land's treasures is the Scarlet Ku'Resh: a hulking, flawless garnet said to grant wishes.

The gem rests in a crumbling shrine, corrupted with caustic black ichor from a desperate, dark bargain made by a forgotten queen.

Can you uncover the shrine's secret, or will you vanish in its depths like so many before you?

Plunderers of the Jeweled Coast is a pulpy 4-page Level 2+ adventure designed for use with Shadowdark RPG.

Includes

  • A tropical pointcrawl through a coastline abuzz with avaricious pirates, where players piece together clues to find the resting place of an ancient queen.
  • A delve through a cursed shrine rife with ancient spirits and the dastardly pirates that disturbed their slumber.
  • A race for lost treasure against the bloodthirsty captain of a dastardly pirate crew: Blackguard Rafael.

Created as part of the Appx. N Jam 2025.

Details

  • System: Shadowdark / OSR
  • Pages: 4
  • Format: PDF
  • Players: 2-5
  • Levels: 2+
  • Playtime: 3 hours
