The Fall of Gutmound

A 10-room fey-orc keep, designed for use with Shadowdark RPG.

Description

The Petalberry Idol has no business being in orcish hands, and the fey won't suffer it. The keep is in chaos: twisted stone, looted riches, and fairies arriving in droves, hungry for mischief.

For a handful of stalwarts, the opportunity is auspicious. Broker with creatures that regard you as prey or mere entertainment, and walk away with your mind intact and coffers filled.

The Fall of Gutmound is a 10-room adventure designed for use with Shadowdark RPG, intended for characters of level 1.

Includes

  • 10 fey-warped rooms described across 2 pages — no prep needed.
  • 4 new monsters, from a maddened fey-orc to a room-filling glue slime.
  • 5 new magic items, including a risky spellcaster's amulet and a bowstring that splits arrows into two homing projectiles.

Details

  • System: Shadowdark / OSR
  • Pages: 8
  • Format: PDF
  • Players: 2-5
  • Levels: 1
  • Playtime: 4+ hours
