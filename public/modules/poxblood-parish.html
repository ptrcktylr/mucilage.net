The Ground Shakes Beneath Poxblood Parish

An 11-room dungeon crawl beneath a remote church, designed for use with Shadowdark RPG.

Description

Tremors around a remote church have locals wary. Hushed whispers and pointed gestures speak of a hellish holdout from the crusade of St. Terragnis that took place there centuries ago: a gargantuan flesh-thing the likes of which the world hasn't seen since.

The behaviour of the ageing priest is increasingly bizarre, and a lay sister suspects the nightly disturbances from the church basement are somehow related.

Can the party unearth the vulgar history lurking below before they become livestock for a power-hungry sorcerer?

The Ground Shakes Beneath Poxblood Parish is an 11-room dungeon crawl designed for use with Shadowdark RPG, intended for characters of level 3 and above.

Includes

  • An aboveground investigation section and a dungeon with two rival factions: a profane cult and a desperate kobold tribe.
  • Several new monsters and NPCs, including a fiendish drow sorcerer and a cryptid-inspired flesh-god that claws at itself for sensation.
  • 7 new magic items, including a lightning-dipped mace, beguiling robes, and an obi that turns its wearer into a mindless thrall.
  • Environmental hazards: toxic gas exposure, tremors, and rooms dense with danger and opportunity.
  • Pathways for meaningful resolutions, not just rooms for stabbing.
  • Separate player and GM dungeon maps.
  • Locations, scenarios, and art that are drop-in ready for any campaign.

Details

  • System: Shadowdark / OSR
  • Pages: 16
  • Format: PDF
  • Players: 2-5
  • Levels: 3+
  • Playtime: 6+ hours
