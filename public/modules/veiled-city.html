The Veiled City

Explore a forgotten vampire metropolis shrouded in an age of shadows.

Description

Deep beneath the world's surface lies a city lost to time—a vampire metropolis where the sun never reaches and ancient bloodlines still hold dominion. The Veiled City was once a thriving underground civilization, but centuries of isolation have transformed it into a labyrinth of political intrigue, dark magic, and forgotten horrors.

Your party has been drawn into this shadowed realm, whether by accident, ambition, or darker forces. Navigate the blood-soaked politics of vampire houses, uncover the city's buried secrets, and survive the predators that lurk in every shadow. Will you escape with your lives, or will you join the countless souls who have disappeared into the eternal night?

Designed for 3-5 players, character levels 4-6. Compatible with D&D 5e and OSR systems.
