The Last Stand of Castle Wogglerock

A Land of Eem compatible adventure featuring strange factions, light puzzling, and forgotten magic.

Description

When a skittering band of carapaced invaders claims his ancient castle, an eccentric young lord of a tiny island fiefdom magically seals himself in his throne room. It falls on a ragtag band of heroes to free the lord and settle the crisis as they see fit.

Explore forgotten chambers, vie with warring Seabugs, and unearth the long-lost Wand of Wogglerock... before someone else does!

Details

  • System: Land of Eem
  • Pages: 31
  • Format: PDF
  • Players: 1-5
  • Levels: 2+
  • Playtime: 6+ hours
